My story

I made Aliyah from France to Jerusalem at the age of 15 , alone. After obtaining my baccalaureate, I pursued studies in graphic design, earned my degree, and worked in the advertising industry for nearly six years. Following a break to focus on my family life, I acquired new skills in dental radiology and creating reports for various radiographies. As technology advanced, I realized the importance of staying current and sought a career in the high-tech industry that suited my interests. Several people familiar with my personality encouraged me to explore the field of Quality Assurance, and indeed, it aligned perfectly with my character.".

I'm looking for a job that will challenge me every day and help me grow in this field.
